DAILY GUIDELINES TO A HEALTHY LIFESTYLE:

  • 2000kcal (ie. milk, rice, red meat, starches, fish...).
  • 90g of sugar (contained in most aliments).
  • 50g of protein (ie. eggs, chicken breast, oats, yogurts...).
  • <70g of fat (ie. avocados, cheese, dark chocolate, nuts...).
  • 150m of moderate activity (cycling or walking) OR 75m of vigorous activity.
  • Strength exercises for major muscles twice/week.

OTHER FACTORS:

A correlation has been found between obesity and average wealth indicators in different regions.
Genetics is another factor that plays a very important part in affecting obesity.
A region's cultural background, location (determines availability), as well as the development of various localised marketing strategies also influence average obesity levels.
